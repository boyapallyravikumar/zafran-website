Our story

Tradition,
refined.

Zafran was born from a single belief: Indian cuisine deserves the same reverence as any great cuisine in the world.

Our founder Chef Arjun Mehta grew up in his grandmother's kitchen in Lucknow, trained in London, and brought it all home to one table — no shortcuts, no compromise.

Our spices are roasted and ground each morning. Breads go into the tandoor to order. Every plate is a conversation between the past and the present.

Chef Arjun Mehta
Founder & Head Chef
